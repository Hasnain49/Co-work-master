These serviced offices in London’s Chancery Lane enjoy a premier location in a modern, glass-fronted building in the centre of Holborn. With numerous corporate headquarters in the vicinity, the area is home to many big players in the legal, research, technology, communications and media sectors. The office space has a convivial reception area to greet you and your guests and there are three easy to book meeting rooms seating up to 16 delegates each. There is a sandwich service on offer to eat at your desk and if work over-runs the centre has 24/7 access.
